Gail Cohn

In loving memory

Daughter, Sister, Wife, Mother, Grandmother, Great-Grandmother
1930-2022

Gail Cohn was born April 19, 1930 in Chicago, Illinois to Mildred E. Schurman and Maurice Wecker. She had 1 younger brother, Michael. She and her brother were raised by her mother and grandparents (Lazar and Mary Schurman) after her parents' divorce.

Gail Cohn was active in her temple youth group. As a young teenager she did some modeling. She graduated highschool as valedictorian. Gail Cohn went to an in-state teacher college. Upon graduation she married her longtime boyfriend Marvin Cohn.

In 1951 after their marriage, Gail and Marvin moved to Baltimore where she started to teach elementary. In 1955 Gail and Marvin purchased a home in the Pikesville community of Baltimore, Maryland. There, they had two kids. Alan Schurman Cohn was born November 7, 1955, followed by Enid Ann Cohn on June 15, 1957.

Both of their children moved out in the 70's and went on to create their own families. Their grandkids by Alan came in mid-late 80's; Joshua, Alexis, and Madision. Their grandkids by Enid came in the early 90's; Jessica and Jason.

Around the turn of the century, as empty nesters and retirees, with their daughter already in Florida with her family, they rented part time, before eventually selling their Baltimore home and moving full time to a townhome at Boca Lago in Boca Raton, Florida. They had active physical and social life including tennis, bridge, and international travel often annually.

In the 2010's they were blessed with the arrival of 7 grandkids; Hudson, Sawyer, and Sage via Alexis Brotspie; Declan and Poppy via Joshua Cohn; Kase and Ryann via Madison Portney. They lived out the rest of their days at Sinai Residences.
